H_a(u^{(a)})&=&\frac{1}{a}\sum_{i=1}^N
\left( u_i^{(a)}-u_{i-1}^{(a)}\right) ^2+a\sum_{i=1}^{N-1}\phi_i^{(a)}u_i^{(a)}\\
